Serve with organic ingredients and taste flavors that are fresh from the Earth. Add or replace what your heart desires. I, personally like to play around with vegeatable choices.

Ingredients

  • Half a table spoon of crushed garlic
  • Tablespoon of raw grated ginger
  • 5 stalks of celery
  • 5 button mushrooms
  • Half a large head of broccoli
  • 1/4 head of cauliflower
  • lemon (optional)
  • 1/5 block of hard tofu-grated
  • 1/2 cup of liquid amines/soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of veggie mince
  • Wonton/dumpling wrappers
  • Soy sauce for dipping
  • Bamboo Steamer

Directions

  1. Chop all vegetable finely and place in a large pot
  2. Cook vegetables until they are soft and liquid is in the bottom of the pot. Take the pot off the heat and let it cool down
  3. While vegetables are cooling, prepare wonton wrappers. Set up a clean plate and small bowl of water
  4. Place a wrapper onto the place. Dip your finger in the bowl of water. Slightly wet the edges. When edges are wet, place the sides together and pinch the sides together in a forward motion
  5. Place the dumplings in the steamer unil soft to the touch and become transparent
  6. Place cooked dumpings on a serving plate with soy sauce mixed with lemon for a tangy sauce
